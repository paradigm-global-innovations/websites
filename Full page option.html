About Us


Paradigm Global Education was founded in 2016 with expertise in corporate technology implementations and training solutions. Today, we are establishing training centers around the world with a focus on emerging technology training, soft skills and leadership development training. We are headquartered in London, U.K., and conduct training within Africa, Europe, The Middle East and U.S.A.

Courses and Specialties


Applied Technologies in Practice

Virtual Reality

Using game engines, 3D tools and state of the art technologies to develop educational content of your choice, this course will teach you how to develop virtual worlds using virtual reality technology. This course is designed to give you an understanding of this evolving technology, developing the skills you need in applying it directly into your discipline.

Augmented Reality

In this course, you will also develop the skills needed to create augmented reality content, interacting with the virtual and real worlds simultaneously. You will develop content and applications in your discipline using state of the art technologies, and applying the knowledge directly to your field of work.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is progressing rapidly, and the current AI systems in place are designed to perform narrow single tasks. Though the goal of AI in the future is to develop stronger AI, it is essential to understand the basics of AI and this technology can apply to several disciplines and be used for beneficial aspects of many disciplines. In this course, you will learn how to incorporate and apply basic AI principles in your discipline.

Web Applications Development

The Web Applications Development course helps learners develop the skills necessary in the creation of application programs that reside on remote servers and are delivered to user devices via internet connections. This course covers mark up and programming languages such as HTML 5, CSS, and JavaScript to develop and deploy web applications (web app) through networks, and web browsers.

Data Analytics and Business Intelligence Using Tableau and Power BI

With the rapid growth and need of accurate data analysis, visualization and intrpretation, this three part course offers an in-depth and practical approach to understanding data, using two of the industry standard tools. In this course, you will learn how to use Tableau and Power BI to harness the power of data analysis and visualization and how this can be applied within your discipline.

Digital Learning Development & Instructional Design

With the continuous growth of digital learning content at all levels of education, this course focuses on the concepts of instructional design, and an in-depth understanding of the aspects that make up the development of effective and inclusive digital learning content.

Animation and Multimedia

2D Animation

In this course, learners will dive into a series of projects specially designed to get you into the animation process instantly. Using industry standard hardware and software, Beginning the foundation, learners will get into the initial principles of animation, and then proceed to advanced practices such as inking, coloring, lighting, etc. As a project-based course, learners will animate their own short movie.

Soft Skills and Leadership Development Training

Today’s professional world needs individuals who bring new perspectives, new thinking and new ideas – and who have the soft skills and knowledge to self-manage in an uncertain world. Our soft skills and leadership training focuses on leadership as a lifelong journey that begins with self-awareness. Our experts from all around the world delivers a wide range of programmes for individuals, managers and senior executives. The topics we deliver are catered specifically to the audience. Examples of such topics include critical thinking, self management, communications, self-leadership, and many more.
